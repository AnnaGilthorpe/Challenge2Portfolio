About Me

I live in London in the UK. I have worked as a TV actress, singer/ songwriter, voiceover artist and teacher for the last 15 years. I am co-founder to two award winning businesses (Tootsie Rollers and Musical Dots) in the entertainment and education sectors. I have always been incredibly interested in tech - my band members call me 'Techy Tootsie' - and love to learn new skills. Historically this has been me teaching myself on my laptop until recently I decided to take the plunge and fully immerse myself in a coding course. I am enjoying the challenge in a new industry and can't wait to collaborate with others and get creative.
